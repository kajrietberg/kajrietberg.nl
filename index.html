Kaj's Front-end Life

After many years working as a special needs teacher of children with autism, pdd-nos, asperger, behavioral problems, I started half 2013 as a trainee Front-end developer to follow my dream and work on/with the internet.
After studying a lot and working hard as a trainee I got my first job as a front-end developer at Zorgweb since april 2014. Learned myself HTML and CSS and I really liked it. In 2017 I started learning JavaScript. I like being challenged and learn new things.

Important things I learned in this period are: "One is never to old to change jobs and learn something new" and "If you know what you want, you can get there".

Grunt, Sass, Jekyll and Jira are tools in which I know my way around. I'm specialized in and like working on/with HTML, CSS, CSS-architecture, BEM and mail-templating. And would like to work on projects that involve Animation, Data-visualisation, CSS, HTML and the web-browser.

Beside my job at Zorgweb I like to work with designers to build beautiful websites and data-visualizations.
So if you would like your design of a website or data-visualization be build by an experienced front-end developer, let me help you and contact me! By phone: +316 - 11955688 or by mail: kaj@kajrietberg.nl.
