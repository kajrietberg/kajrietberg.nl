Kaj's Front-end Life

I started half 2013 as a trainee Front-end developer to follow my dream and work on/with the internet. Before that I was a special needs teacher working with children with autism, pdd-nos, asperger, behavioral problems.
After studying a lot and working hard as a trainee I got my first job as a front-end developer at Zorgweb since april 2014. Learned myself HTML and CSS and I really liked it.

Important things I learned are: "One is never to old to change jobs and learn something new" and "If you know what you want, you can get there".

I'm specialized in HTML, CSS, CSS-architecture and mail-templating. Grunt, Sass and Jira are tools in which I know my way around. In 2017 I started with learning JavaScript after a few earlier attempts.
I'm interested in Animation, Data-visualisation, CSS, HTML and the web-browser.

Beside my job at Zorgweb I like to work with designers to build beautiful websites and data-visualizations.
So if you would like your design of a website or data-visualization be build by an experienced front-end developer, let me help you and contact me!
